This is the recipe to make the best Lasagna!

Lasagna is a delicious and popular Italian dish that consists of alternating layers of flat pasta sheets, savory meat or vegetable fillings, and rich, creamy cheese sauce. The pasta sheets are typically pre-cooked before being layered with ingredients like ground meat, such as beef or sausage, saut√©ed vegetables, such as onions, bell peppers, and mushrooms, and a variety of cheeses, such as mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta. These layers are then assembled in a baking dish and baked until the pasta is tender and the cheese on top is golden and bubbly. Lasagna is known for its hearty and comforting flavors, making it a favorite among pasta lovers worldwide. It is often served as a main course for family gatherings, special occasions, or enjoyed as leftovers for subsequent meals.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion & Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices & Seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna sheets: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna sheets.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Eggs: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

How to cook the lasagna step-by-step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the sheets.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to layer the lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. lasagna sheets
  3. Ricotta mixture.
  4. Mozzarella slices.
  5. Meat sauce.
  6. Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
